*********
Basemaps
*********

:Author: Thomas Bonfort
:Contact: tbonfort at terriscope.fr

:Author: Yves Jacolin
:Contact: yjacolin at free.fr

:Author: Nicolas Ribot

Basemaps project is a mapfile for MapServer in order to generate maps from
OpenStreetMaps data in different styles like Google, Bing or using custom
style.

.. toctree::
   :maxdepth: 1

   install
   style

Features
---------

* Service WMS
* Colored and Grey level maps
* Custom style
* World or specific extent data
* Standard or custom projections
